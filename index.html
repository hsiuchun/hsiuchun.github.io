Hsiu-Chun Tsai

I received the BBA degree in the Department of Information Management, National University of Kaohsiung (NUK), Taiwan, in 2022, and now is a master student in the Department of Information Management, National Central University (NCU), Taiwan.
I was a member of the Computational Biology & Intelligence System Lab supervised by Prof. Tzu-Hsien Yang during my undergraduate studies, and published three journal papers when I worked as a research assistant for Dr. Yang. Now I am a member of Advanced Data Mining & Learning (ADML) Laboratory supervised by Prof. Yi-Cheng Chen, which are interested in machine learning, social network analysis, data mining, etc.
My research interests include deep learning (DL) and natural language processing (NLP).
